About Us

Tech-Enabled Multicultural Buying Group

We certify and connect diverse suppliers with corporate and government buyers, creating equitable access to high-value contracts and global supply chains.

Through integrated sourcing, logistics, financing, and U.S.-aligned trade partnerships, we streamline procurement while boosting opportunities for minority- and women-owned businesses across Latin America, the Caribbean, the Middle East, China, and beyond.
