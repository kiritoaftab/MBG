Who we are

Empowering Multicultural Commerce

MBG bridges global brands with multicultural communities, using technology, cultural insight, and collective buying power to drive inclusive growth.

To empower multicultural communities worldwide by providing access to global products, fair pricing, and opportunities that foster inclusion and growth.

To become the leading multicultural buying group, redefining cross-border commerce and creating an ecosystem where brands and communities thrive together.

Founded with the idea of collective buying, MBG has evolved into a tech-enabled platform, connecting global brands with diverse markets and enabling inclusive growth since its inception.
